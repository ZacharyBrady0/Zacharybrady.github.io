Steak n' Blue

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
4 pieces Beef tenderloin, gorgonzola, heavy cream, black pepper. 24oz tenderloin, 10oz gorgonzola, 8oz heavy cream, black pepper to taste

Step 1: Get a 12" cast iron skillet hot. Preheat oven to 350F.
Step 2: Salt tenderloins, place in skillet once skillet is hot.
Step 3: Cook for 3 mins, then flip. Cook for 3 more mins.
Step 4: Transfer skillet with steaks still in it to the oven. Cook 5 mins or longer, depending on temperature desired.
Step 5: In a small skillet, place gorgonzola and heavy cream and reduce. Crack pepper over cream until desired flavor is reached.
Step 6: Remove steaks from oven, and then from the pan. Rest steaks 5 mins.
Step 7: Cover steaks with sauce.
